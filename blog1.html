Exclusive Eggnog Recipe

  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup rum (optional)

Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, whisk until smooth, and chill for at least 2 hours. Serve with a sprinkle of nutmeg on top for that extra cozy touch!

Baking Tips

  • Always preheat your oven for even baking.
  • Measure ingredients accurately for best results.
  • Use room temperature eggs and butter for better mixing.
  • Don’t open the oven door too often; it can cause temperature fluctuations.
